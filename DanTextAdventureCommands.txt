Dan Bowen's Text Adventure commands help.  The unique commands for my games may be spoilers for puzzles!   Rev 2020-12-07

Common commands:
   North     South     East      West      Up      Down
   Enter     Look      Examine   Search    Eat     Drink
   Open      Close     Wait      Move      Inventory
   Get       Drop      Tell      Turn	   
   
   The most common commands can usually be shortened to single letters, for example N, S, E, W for directional commands, I for Inventory, X for Examine etc.  Wait is abbreviated as Z.

   
   Out of game commands:
      Save      Restore    Undo    Quit


Unique commands in my games:
Insert  Follow
